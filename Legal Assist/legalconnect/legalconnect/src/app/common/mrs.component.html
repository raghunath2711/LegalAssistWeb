Eviction in Washington State

For assistance with eviction go to Washington State 211 or 2-1-1 from a landline, 206-461-3200 or 800-621-4636 or 206-461-3610 for TTY/hearing impaired calls. The staff will tell you about agencies that can help you. They can also refer you to other resources such as financial education classes.
